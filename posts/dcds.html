photos from a faraway world

Ive always loved how crunchy 3ds photos come out. They come out like you've been sent photos from the lunar rover exploring the moon, or that submarine exploring the depths in that game "Iron Lung". I realized after taking some photos of Washingtons most famous landmarks that it invokes that feeling of exploring a distant and haunted world. I hope one day I can come back to DC and feel that sense of discovery, a feeling I wish I felt my first time to our nations capital - or maybe even feel some kind of pride.
