angels and demons movie

rating: [**---]

Reccommended by Madelynns step dad to watch as we all sat in the living room. Its definitely one of those "dad" movies - and no shade either. It is what it is lol.

Fun fact - this movie had to make a website to explain to the public that the "science" in the movie is all fiction and doesnt pose a threat. No, gam gam, terrorists arent going to CERN to steal some antimatter and use it to destroy us.

This wouldnt be the only time CERN enters the spotlight of mass conspiracy theories - so it was interesting to see it in a hollywood film. Thats where the fun stops for me, mostly.

The plot is predictable. Tom Hanks delivers a cardboard performance of his symbology genius character, Robert Langdon. The assassin that drives the plot forward refuses to kill Robert because...he wasnt instructed to and it would be a sin. Disregard the pile of corpses this guy leaves in his wake wherever he goes. How convenient! I never got the sense that their were any stakes for our MC.

I learned what conclave was though, so that was cool.
